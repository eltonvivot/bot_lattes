2007 - 2009
FAPESP TIDIA-Ae (fase 2) Aprendizado Eletronico sobre Plataforma de Redes de Alta Velocidade
Descrição: This project proposes the participation of Interlab, Interactive Technologies Laboratory, at Escola Politécnica of USP, as LDA in "Projeto TIDIA Ae - FASE II". For this purpose, besides acting collaboratively in software R&D activities, as later appointed by the project's Management Comitee, Interlab is presenting proposals of exploratory R&D activities and of development of learning activities. The first consists in the development of an experimental teleconference system enriched with augmented reality technology, integrating a real lecturer (video-avatar) in a three-dimensional virtual environment in real time, allowing studies and experiments in interface solutions, in new learning applications and in the integration of this new technology with the TIDIA/Ae system. The latter proposes the creation of a virtual interactive course on 3D omputer graphics on the TIDIA/Ae platform, based on Java 3D and game development technology, and using tools developed at Interlab and currently hosted at FAPESP's "TIDIA Incubadora Virtual". These tools are Interlab 3D and enJine. Situação: Concluído; Natureza: Pesquisa.

2020 - Atual
COLOSSAL: Collaboration on Flow across scales (Brazil, France, Norway, USA)
Descrição: Use of Lévy statistics theory and H-theory to approach porous and fractured media that typically span many orders of magnitude in length scales. New theoretical ideas are needed to address this challenge. A recently developed statistical framework proposed by the partners in Brazil, the H-theory, has the potential to shed light into the issue. Through collaborations with universities in Brazil, France, and USA, we will have access to unique time series of flow in fractured rocks and earthquake catalogues on which we could test new statistical and numerical models.. Situação: Em andamento; Natureza: Pesquisa. Integrantes: Ernesto Carneiro Pessoa Raposo - Integrante / A. M. S. Macêdo - Integrante / François Renard - Coordenador / Marcel Moura - Integrante / Giovani L. Vasconcelos - Integrante.

2005 - 2009
SCIENTIFIC INVESTIGATION OF THE ST-R-1 REQUIREMENTS APPLIED IN THE SAFE TRANSPORT OF NATURALLY OCCURRING RADIOACTIVE MATERIAL (NORM)
Descrição: The aim of this work is  assist the IAEA in the development of a radiological basis for the establishment of transport requirements applicable to naturally occurring radioactive material, in particular a more realistic method to determine transport indexes (TI) and surface dose rates in the case of NORM consignments taking into consideration the percentage of uranium / thorium (in ppm) in the NORM as well as the size of the load.. Situação: Em andamento; Natureza: Pesquisa. Integrantes: Paulo Fernando Lavalle Heilbron Filho - Coordenador.

CIRCLE
Descrição: The area of Molecular Communications seeks to leverage biological phenomenon at the molecular levelsuch as inter cell communication, usage of immune system, bacteria DNA transfer, neuronal signaling and calcium signaling to build a communications infrastructure for nano-scale devices that can be deployed and coordinate sophisticated operations within biological systems such as the human body. Within this highly multidisciplinary area, disparate islands of research are emerging with little coordination. The CIRCLE project, which has been funded by the European Commission, aims to harmonize these islands of research in Molecular Communications across Europe by providing a structured research agenda to accelerate convergence and advance breakthrough research within the area.. Application of Control Theory in Molecular Communication for the Treatment of Alzheimer?s Disease
Descrição: Molecular communications is a new paradigm that enables nanomachines to communicate within a biological environment. One form of molecular communications is calcium (Ca2+) signalling, which occurs naturally in living biological cells. This process is important for many regulatory systems inside the human body and thus it is very important to understand this for both biology and medical purposes. Therefore, modelling and analysis was performed using communication theory tools to model biological communication system.
 
Ca2+ signalling enables cells in a tightly packed tissue structure to communicate at short ranges with neighbouring cells. Ca2+ ions then diffuse through a physical connection between cells called a gap junction. Such signalling can be found in different classes of cell. In excitable cells, initiation of the Ca2+-signalling process is accompanied by an electrical component; for non-excitable cell types, the electrical component is absent; while hybrid cells exhibit both behaviours. A comparison and analysis of the communication behaviour, is our first focus, in tissues comprised of three specific cell types that utilize Ca2+ signalling, namely: epithelium cells (non-excitable), smooth muscle cells (excitable), and astrocytes (hybrid). The analysis focuses on spatiotemporal Ca2+ concentration dynamics and how they are influenced by the intracellular signalling process, the molecular diffusion delay, the gain and capacity of the communication channel, as well as intracellular signalling interference. Also, the proposed communication system was studied based on the impact that the deformation of the tissue structure has on the communication channel. This lead to a proposed process for nanomachines utilizing Ca2+ based molecular communication to infer and detect the state of the tissue, which we term as the Molecular Nanonetwork Inference Process. This analysis of the communication behavior in the context of tissues provided insights useful for, inter alia, the design of nanomachines that are situated within tissues and analysis of the communication channel to infer tissue health.

CogNet
Descrição: CogNet will develop solutions to provide a higher and more intelligent level of automated monitoring and management of networks and applications, improve operational efficiencies and facilitate the requirements of 5G.
The project will conduct and exploit leading research in the areas of data gathering, machine learning, data analytics and autonomic network management. The ultimate objective is to enable the larger and more dynamic network topologies necessary in 5G, improve the end-user QoS, and to lower capital and operational costs through improved efficiencies and the use of node, link and function virtualisation.. Laboratório para Cidades Inteligentes
Descrição: O objetivo desse projeto é a criação de um laboratório para o desenvolvimento de pesquisas relacionadas ao tema
Cidades Inteligentes. A principal infraestrutura necessária para viabilizar o LCI é a implantação de uma ou mais redes de
computadores metropolitanas que permitam a instalação de qualquer tipo de sensor ou atuador em qualquer ponto do
município de Campina Grande. A potencial redundância das redes metropolitanas do LCI tem o objetivo de disponibilizar
uma maior capacidade de experimentação, possibilitando o uso de diferentes tecnologias, isoladamente ou associadas, e
até mesmo a comparação da maior ou menor adequação dessas tecnologias para uma determinada aplicação.
Complementa a infraestrutura do LCI um sistema computacional de retaguarda para armazenamento e processamento da
enorme quantidade de dados que será gerada pelos sensores conectados às estruturas da rede metropolitana do LCI,
específicos de cada projeto de Pesquisa, Desenvolvimento e Inovação (PD&I) a ser executado sobre a infraestrutura
compartilhada do LCI. O LCI se apoiará e ampliará substancialmente a infraestrutura da Rede Metropolitana de Campina
Grande (Metro-CG). A Metro-CG é parte da iniciativa Redes Comunitárias de Educação e Pesquisa (Redecomep), um
projeto do Ministério da Ciência e Tecnologia (MCT) coordenado pela Rede Nacional de Ensino e Pesquisa (RNP/MCT).. Situação: Concluído; Natureza: Pesquisa. 2009 - 2010
Uma arquitetura de redes veiculares voltada para segurança no trânsito
Descrição: Como há uma demanda crescente de estudos em torno das VANETS(Redes Veiculares) vimos interesse de desenvolver um trabalho de pesquisa neste valioso tema. Consideramos o mais importante sub-tema uma maneira de fazer os carros se comunicarem a fins de evitar acidentes e fazer do trânsito automotivo um lugar mais seguro possível. Resolvemos então desenvolver uma arquitetura voltada pra a segurança do trânsito para que seja implementado um sistema de ajuda ao condutor a trafegar em vias mais seguras e também a evitar acidentes e mortes..

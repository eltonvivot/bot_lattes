WELMO - Wearable Electronics for Effective Lung Monitoring
Descrição: The project aims to develop and validate a new generation of low-cost, low-power miniaturized sensors, integrated into a comfortable vest, enabling effective and accurate monitoring of the lungs in real-time through the simultaneous acquisition of electrical impedance tomography ( EIT) and pulmonary sound signals. The consortium is composed of 9 European institu- tions.. Situação: Em andamento; Natureza: Pesquisa. Integrantes: César Alexandre Domingues Teixeira - Coordenador / Rui Paiva - Integrante.

2014 - 2018
ThermoResponse - Thermo-responsive ultrasonographic tissue analysis: towards a new imaging modality
Descrição: This bilateral cooperation project aimed to support the develop- ment of a new form of ultrasound imaging. The modality under development is based on the mapping of intensity variations in conventional ultrasound images, caused by changes in backscattered energy induced by changes in temperature.. Situação: Concluído; Natureza: Pesquisa. Integrantes: César Alexandre Domingues Teixeira - Coordenador.

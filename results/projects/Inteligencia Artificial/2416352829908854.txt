Agricultural land yield and acreage reponses to prices and freight rates in Brazil
Descrição: Land yield as agricultural production per land unit area and acreage are critical for determining the land availability for its various purposes, such as, to provide food, fiber and biofuels to the population. Estimation of agricultural land yield and acreage elasticities with respect to output prices and freight rates are useful to understand the effects of land use change on the environment, food production, and other policy related issues. This research?s objectives are to investigate the conditioners of land yield and agricultural acreage in Brazil and estimate their elasticities. In so doing, we will specify econometric models based on the propositions of a locational rent maximization theoretical model that brings together the von Thünen (1826) and David Ricardo (1817) theories on land use.. 2014 - Atual
Economic and non economic incentives for organic agricultural production in Brazil
Descrição: This is a multidisciplinary and multi-institutional research project that aims at investigating economic and non economic factors, such as psychological and social capital, that may be either incentives or disincentives to conversion to organic agricultural production in Brazil. Those factors are likely to explain the low conversion rate from conventional to organic agricultural production in Brazil. To do so, we use an economic approach that econometrically compares profitability and productivity of conventional to organic production.  We will expand the econometric approach so to use spatial weighting matrices, thus incorporating the effect of farmers proximity in our analyzes.. Situação: Em andamento; Natureza: Pesquisa.

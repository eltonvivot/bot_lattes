Women Building Social and Solidarity Economy through Urban Agricultural Cooperatives in Northeast Brazil: Power Dynamics and Strategic Alliances
Descrição: In line with the Insight program goals, the purpose of this research project is twofold: (1) to enhance an interdisciplinary understanding of Brazilian female small-scale entrepreneurs? initiatives in building social and solidarity-driven economy through local cooperatives in urban agriculture, and (2) to identify the opportunities and challenges offered by such cooperatives to secure these women?s socioeconomic rights, particularly in regards to empowerment outcomes for those of them living in favelas (slums) and/or in marginalized positions. To this end, the proposed project will bring together researchers from Canada and Brazil with expertise in female entrepreneurship, social movements, and gender and development to provide comparative insights into local women?s grassroots experiences, drawing on case studies from urban agriculture in the states of Ceará and Pernambuco, Northeast Brazil.. Situação: Concluído; Natureza: Pesquisa.

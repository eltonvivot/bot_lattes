2017 - 2019
TRAILS TO METASTASIS OR APOPTOSIS: IT TAKES TWO TO DECIDE?
Descrição: TRAIL agonist receptors DR4 and DR5 display divergent signalling capabilities. DR4 being only able to transduce apoptosis, while DR5 in addition, and in some conditions, can transduce cell motility and invasion. The INSERM LNC UMR1231 team has generated Inverted mutants of DR4/DR5 proximal intracellular domain to delineate the motif that allows DR5 to signal cell motility. These DR5 chimeric mutants harbour amino acids corresponding to DR4 and vice versa. This secondment aims on the one hand at evaluating the cell motility/invasion properties of inverted mutants (xCELLigence and Operetta), and on the other at assessing, a priory, putative players of this promigratory signalling pathway, including RAC1 and Src, two kinases suspected to be involved. Structure function analysis also point to a putative PKC zeta phosphorylation, present in DR5 intracellular proximal domain, but absent in DR4. For this aim, we will knock-down the expression of these putative targets using the CRISPR-Cas9 technology that we have set up in our laboratory. To exclude any possibility that known components of the proapoptotic machinery may be involved in this process too, such as RIPK1, or FADD, the latter will also be deleted by CRIPSR-CAS9. INVESTIGAÇÃO DAS VIAS DE MORTE INDUZIDAS PELA SALIVA DO CARRAPATO Amblyomma sculptum SOBRE CÉLULAS TUMORAIS
Descrição: Determinar a amplitude da ação citotóxica da saliva bruta e/ou suas frações obtidas do carrapato Amblyomma sculptum sobre diferentes células humanas tumorais e normais, definindo as características celulares que determinam a sensibilidade ou resistência destas células à morte celular induzida pelos compostos.

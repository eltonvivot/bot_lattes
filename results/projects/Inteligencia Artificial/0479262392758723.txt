2015 - Atual
Influence of the welding thermal cycles on the microstructure of the stainless steel for applications offshore
Projeto certificado pelo(a) coordenador(a) Sidnei Paciornik em 18/10/2017.
Descrição: The main objective of this project is to carry out a systematic research on the effects of large and low heat input on the microstructure and mechanical-corrosive behavior in the heat affected zones of duplex, super duplex and new lean duplex stainless steels. These results will allow establishing the highest acceptable time-temperature conditions for safe operation of these materials in different aggressive environments. Thus, by using controlled thermal cycles, recent thermodynamical methods and novel simulation procedures will be possible to develop scientific and technical basis for optimal welding operations of new grades of DSSs.. 2012 - 2014
Origin and Effects of the Precipitation on the Strengthening Mechanisms in the pipelines steels.
Descrição: We established the correlation between the rolling schedule of a commercial X80 pipeline steel with the microstructure. The strengthening mechanisms taking into account the chemical composition and the phase transformations during the thermomechanical processing of this steel were calculated.   This study from the nano-structure has the aim to understand the effects of later deformations, processes of weld and corrosion resistance of theses API X80 linepipe steels whose technological requirements have been predetermined previously.. 2008 - 2010
Phase Transformations on Tempering in Fe-Based Alloys.
Descrição: The KTOPT is used to characterize the  main nucleation and growth protocols during reactions on tempering in low alloy steels and some Fe-based alloys. The fraction transformed of second phases is calculated for both isotermal and non-isothermal transformations on tempering. TEM and mechanical tests are used to show the fundamental steps that control the precipitation reaction during the thermal treatment.. Situação: Concluído; Natureza: Pesquisa.
